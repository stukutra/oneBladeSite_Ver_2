Why this course?

Become an expert in Data Warehouse design and management! Do you want to learn how to build an efficient and scalable Data Warehouse? Are you a data analyst looking to deepen your knowledge of data integration and optimization techniques? Are you a DBA or IT expert aiming to improve enterprise data management to support Business Intelligence? This course will provide you with an in-depth understanding of Data Warehouse design, management, and optimization, including advanced techniques in **ETL, dimensional modeling, and data security**. Become a reference point in enterprise data management!

Course Modules

  • Definition of Data Warehouse and comparison with operational databases
  • OLTP vs OLAP and Data Warehouse architecture
  • Role of the Data Warehouse in Business Intelligence

  • Conceptual and logical models
  • Star and Snowflake schemas
  • Creation of a dimensional model

  • ETL processes: extraction, transformation, and loading
  • ETL tools and data cleaning techniques

  • Performance tuning
  • Data security and governance management
