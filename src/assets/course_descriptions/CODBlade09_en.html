Why this course?

Become an expert in database management with SQL! Do you want to learn how to effectively manage and manipulate relational databases? Are you a developer looking to optimize your SQL knowledge? Are you a data analyst wanting to improve your skills in extracting and managing information? This course will provide you with an in-depth understanding of the SQL language, from database creation to query optimization. Learn the best practices for working with major RDBMS and take your skills to the next level!

Course Modules

  • Introduction to relational databases
  • Table structure and normalization concepts
  • Introduction to major RDBMS (MySQL, PostgreSQL, SQL Server)

  • Fundamental commands: SELECT, INSERT, UPDATE, DELETE
  • WHERE, JOIN, GROUP BY, HAVING clauses
  • Aggregation functions and subqueries

  • Indexes, triggers, and stored procedures
  • Backup, restore, and security management
  • Query optimization

  • Creation of a database with advanced queries
  • Data analysis and reporting
  • Best practices for database management
