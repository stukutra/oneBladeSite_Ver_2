Why this course?

Learn to drive innovation and digital transformation with Design Thinking! Do you want to understand how to tackle change in a modern company? Are you a manager, entrepreneur, or professional looking to adopt effective strategies for innovation and digital transformation? This course will provide you with practical tools of **Design Thinking, Digital Transformation, and Change Management**, to create innovative solutions, improve Customer Experience, and manage organizational change. Become a protagonist of the digital future!

Course Modules

  • The context of business innovation
  • Why Digital Transformation is crucial for business success
  • Technology, culture, and organizational change

  • Principles and methodology of Design Thinking
  • Empathy, ideation, and prototyping
  • Case studies and practical applications

  • Managing organizational change
  • Strategies for effective change management
  • Overcoming resistance to change
